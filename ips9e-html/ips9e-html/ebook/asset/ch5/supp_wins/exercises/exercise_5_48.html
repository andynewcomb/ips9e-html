5.48 Free-throw shooting. April is a college basketball player who makes 80% of her free throws. In a recent game, she had 10 free throws and missed three of them. How unusual is this outcome? Using software, calculator, or Table C, compute 1 − P(X ≤ 2), where X is the number of free throws missed in 10 shots. Explain your answer.
