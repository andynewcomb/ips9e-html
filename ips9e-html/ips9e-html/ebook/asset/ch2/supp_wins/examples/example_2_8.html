Laundry detergents. Consumers Union provides ratings on a large variety of consumer products. They use sophisticated testing methods as well as surveys of their members to create these ratings. The ratings are published in their magazine, Consumer Reports.4

LAUNDRY

One recent study rated 53 laundry detergents on a scale from 1 to 100. The scale summarizes washing performance under a variety of conditions. Price per load is given in cents.5 We will examine the relationship between rating and price per load for these laundry detergents. We expect that the higher-priced detergents will tend to have higher ratings.
