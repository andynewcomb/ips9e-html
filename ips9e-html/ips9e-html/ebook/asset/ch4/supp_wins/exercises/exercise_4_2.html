4.2 Are these phenomena random? Identify each of the following phenomena as random or not. Give reasons for your answers.

  1. (a) The outside temperature in your town at noon on Groundhog Day, February 2.

  2. (b) The first digit in your student identification number.

  3. (c) You draw an ace from a well-shuffled deck of 52 cards.
