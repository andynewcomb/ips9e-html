4.4 Are the trials independent? For each of the following situations, identify the trials as independent or not. Explain your answers.

  1. (a) You record the outside temperature in your town at noon on Groundhog Day, February 2, each year for the next five years.

  2. (b) The number of tweets that you receive on the next 10 Mondays.
