Voter records show that 56% of registered voters had actually voted. We will see in the next chapter that, in this situation, the proportion of the sample who voted has approximately the Normal distribution with mean μ = 0.56 and standard deviation σ = 0.019.

  1. (a) If the respondents answer truthfully, what is ? This is the probability that estimates 0.56 within plus or minus 0.04.

  2. (b) In fact, 72% of the respondents said they had voted (). If respondents answer truthfully, what is ? This probability is so small that it is good evidence that some people who did not vote claimed that they did vote.
